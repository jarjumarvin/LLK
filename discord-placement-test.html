Placement Test

Ready to test your Korean skills? We have a Placement Test for you to see where you are.

It has three portions and will take about 45 minutes to complete.
This three-part exam will test your speaking, reading comprehension, and writing through prompts and multiple choice questions.
When you are ready, PM a Mentor (who is online and active) to take the test.

We recommend at least six months of studying before you take the test.

Students who are placed into “Advanced” have typically studied Korean for about 2-3 years.
There are three placements:

씨앗 (Beginner) - about 0-40% correct
새싹 (Intermediate) - about 40-77% correct
나무 (Advanced) - about 77%+ correct

This is meant for you to assess your knowledge and receive feedback from mentors. Don’t be afraid to see where you are - even an 나무 (Advanced) level student can improve their Korean knowledge, and we hope that this is an opportunity for beginner level students to receive feedback and grow. :-)
